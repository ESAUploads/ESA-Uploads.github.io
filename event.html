Young Leader Yakubu Tia Fatawu Announces Candidacy for NUGS President - UDS (NYC)

With a clear vision for the future of the National Union of Ghana Students (NUGS) at the University for Development Studies (UDS), Nyankpala Campus (NYC), Yakubu Tia Fatawu has officially declared his candidacy for the position of NUGS President. Fatawu, a dedicated member of the UDS community, outlined his intentions in a passionate Declaration of Intent:

Yakubu's passion for student advocacy and his commitment to positive change are truly inspiring. His decision to vie for this national leadership role speaks volumes about the caliber of students we nurture here. Let's rally behind Yakubu as he embarks on this important journey to represent the voice of Ghanaian students on a national platform. We wish him the very best in the upcoming NUGS elections!

"Dear Esteemed Colleagues, With a heart full of passion, a mind brimming with ideas and a spirit fueled by service, I, YAKUBU TIA FATAWU, humbly and determinedly declare my candidature for NUGS PRESIDENT -UDS (NYC). As a proud member of the Baobab family, I have witnessed firsthand our challenges, the overlooked opportunities and the great potentials we possess. My vision is to foster academic excellence, student welfare, and inclusive leadership. I am not just seeking a position, but an opportunity to serve, learn, lead and make a lasting impact. Together, we can build the UDS (NYC) we all envision." Fatawu, who is being referred to as "The Youngest Professor" by his team, emphasizes a platform centered on student-driven advocacy and a commitment to being ready to serve. His declaration signals the start of what is expected to be a closely watched election for the leadership of NUGS-UDS (NYC). Students are eager to hear more about his specific plans and how he intends to achieve his stated goals of academic excellence, student welfare, and inclusive leadership within the university community.
