Yakubu Tia Fatawu Eyes NUGS Presidency

Exciting news from our faculty! We are thrilled to announce that one of our very own bright minds, Yakubu Tia Fatawu, a dedicated Level 300 student, is exploring candidacy for the prestigious position of President of the National Union of Ghana Students (NUGS)!

Yakubu's passion for student advocacy and his commitment to positive change are truly inspiring. His decision to vie for this national leadership role speaks volumes about the caliber of students we nurture here. Let's rally behind Yakubu as he embarks on this important journey to represent the voice of Ghanaian students on a national platform. We wish him the very best in the upcoming NUGS elections!

As he prepares for this role, he seeks your votes and support to amplify student voices across Ghana.
